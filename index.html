About Me

Originally from Dominican Republic and currently settled in Kissimmee FL, my name is Eric Patricio and I am an aspiring Front-End Web Developer.

I have over 5 years of experience in Warehouse Operations and Management. However, the recent COVID-19 pandemic has challenged me to broaden my knowledge and skillset which led me to study and acquire the requisite skills to become a Front-End Developer.

I would describe myself as being passionate about tech and coding from scratch. Additionally, I enjoy bringing ideas to life for the web, with a fundamental understanding of mobile-first design concepts and problem solving. I am constantly on a path of learning and focusing on my long-term objective to become a Full Stack Developer.

I enjoy every minute with my family, playing with my kiddos, I like to invent different dishes that I sometimes see on the web and cook it.
